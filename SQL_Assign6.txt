Assignment 6
Using Special operator in condition

1)Write two different queries that would produce all orders taken on odtober 3rd or 4th 1990?
Ans -:	1)select * from orders where odate = '1990-10-03' or odate='1990-10-04';
	2)select *from orders where odate between '1990-10-03' and '1990-10-04';

2)Write a query that selects all of the customers serviced by peel or motika.(Hint:The snum field relates the two tables to one another)
Ans -:	1)select * from customers where Snum in( select Snum from salespeople where sname in ('peel', 'Motika'));
	2)select *from CUSTOMERS where (Snum=1001 OR Snum=1004);

3)Write a query that will produce all the customers whose names begine with the latter 'C'?
Ans -: select *from customers where cname>='A' and cname<'H';

4)write a query that selects all customers whose name begine with latter 'C'?
Ans -:select * from customers where cname like'c%';

5)Write a query that selects all orders except those with zeroes or NULLs in the amt field.
Ans -:	1)select *from orders where amt>0;
	2)select *from orders where amt<>0 or amt<>NULL;
